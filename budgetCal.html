Budget Planner

A tool used to determine that the amount of budget that the user can afford. Based on the personal financial situation, economic environment and housing requirements to generate a customized plan for the homebuyers. The system will use an questionnaire format for the user to input their conditions. There are 5 stages for evaluating the user's financial situation and personal requirements.

It will take about 10-15 minutes for answering the question.
